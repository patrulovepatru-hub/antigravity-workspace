# Web3 Bug Bounty Platforms

## Top Platforms

### Immunefi (immunefi.com)
- Largest Web3 bug bounty platform
- Payouts up to $10M+
- Focus: DeFi, bridges, L2s
- Notable programs: Wormhole, MakerDAO, Polygon

### Code4rena (code4rena.com)
- Competitive audits
- Time-limited contests
- Prize pools $50k-$1M+
- Good for learning from other hunters

### Sherlock (sherlock.xyz)
- Audit contests
- Lead Senior Watson rankings
- Insurance-backed protocols

### Hats Finance (hats.finance)
- Decentralized bug bounty
- On-chain submissions

### Direct Programs
- Uniswap, Aave, Compound have direct programs
- Check protocol docs/security pages

## Vulnerability Severity (typical)

| Severity | Payout Range | Examples |
|----------|--------------|----------|
| Critical | $50k - $10M+ | Fund theft, protocol insolvency |
| High | $10k - $50k | Significant fund loss, DoS |
| Medium | $1k - $10k | Limited fund loss, griefing |
| Low | $100 - $1k | Informational, best practices |

## Common High-Value Targets
- Bridges (cross-chain vulnerabilities)
- Lending protocols (liquidation bugs)
- DEXs (price manipulation)
- Yield aggregators (strategy exploits)
